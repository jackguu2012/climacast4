ClimaCast Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

At ClimaCast, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal data you provide.

What We Collect

  • Name
  • Email address (for signing in)
  • Region or general location (to personalise your forecast)

What We Don’t Do

  • ❌We do not sell your data
  • ❌We do not track you for advertising
  • ❌We do not store precise location without your permission

Why We Collect It

  • To personalise your weather forecasts
  • To manage your user account
  • To improve the ClimaCast experience

Cookies

ClimaCast may use essential cookies to keep you securely signed in. We do not use any third-party tracking cookies.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Ask us to correct or delete your data

To do this, email us at: help@climacast.uk

Under 13?

ClimaCast is not intended for children under 13. We don’t knowingly collect data from anyone under that age.

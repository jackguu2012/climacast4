Thunderstorm Warnings Issued

Author: Newsroom, Written: 13/06/2025

Thunderstorm warnings have been issued today, in effect overnight Friday and into Saturday.

Thunderstorms are developing rapidly over the North of France, due to hot and humid weather over the continent.

"These developing storms may bring heavy downpours and large hailstones along with frequent lightning," says the Met Office.


Further warnings may be issued for Saturday stretching up to Scotland and covering North England.
